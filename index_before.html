Hi everyone, welcome to my website. This website is made as an attempt for me to learn the super power of web development, mostly to make myself happy by being able to showcase myself on the internet. It took me a really long time to realize that the desire to "perform" and "showcase", through various artistic mediums, myself to the world is the one thing that makes me truly happy. I am hoping that this motivation lives on for both myself and other like-minded creative people.

Story so far ...

I was born in a relatively populous city called Anshan, LiaoNing Province, PRC. Our city was known for it's production of Iron and Steel established during the 1950s' "Great Leap Forward" and 1960s' "Cultural Revolution", and a Jade Buddha.
On Christmas Day 2001, I arrived in Mississauga, ON, CA with my parents who were skilled-worker immigrants.
I currently attend the University of Waterloo, in the co-op Mathematics/Teaching program. In addition to my collection of courses in pure and discrete mathematics, I'm also attempting a major in Computer Science.
I attended Queen's University for Teacher's College from 2012-2013, during which I had some meaningful practicum experiences, as well as took a course in Aboriginal Education that changed the scope of my world forever.
I will graduate with a Bachelors of Mathematics and Bachelors of Education in 2015.
In the past, I have worked as a student teacher at public schools and private schools, locally and abroad:
  • Kitchener Collegiate Institute
  • Trinity College School
  • Atlanta International School
I also had a stint teaching Math Cirlces for the Center for Education in Mathematics and Computing, the organization that I admired very much from various Math Contests back in high school.

Nearly all my hobbies involve expressing art in some form. I used to draw manga-styled art, traditional art, play cello and violin, sing Chinese folk songs, practice performance Kung-Fu, and recently I've started down the path of creative writing.
Even more recently, I've started to take a liking to writing code and make computers do magical things for expression, thus leading to the birth of this website.
There will be more projects of mine to come, and this website will grow with me. Thanks for watching over me, and stay tuned!
