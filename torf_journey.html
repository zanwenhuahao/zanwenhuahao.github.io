TORF Journey


The Teachers' Overseas Recruiting Fair held at Queen's University each year is a great job fair. I highly recommend it to teachers in Canada who are interested in pursuing international teaching, especially for starting-out teachers and teachers going abroad for the first time. I found my job at the International School of London Qatar through TORF this year.
